FEATURE

Create pictures which have words composed of dots. 

Easy

The user interface of Dottarry is quite simple so that you can easily create your own Dottarry within several mouse clicks! 

Creative

We provide several tools for you to design your own Dottarry. Just dot your inspiration and let your mind fly!

Fantastic

Have you ever seen alphabets and punctuation consists of dots? If not, all you can discover here is cool and fun and fantastic works!

We Provide...

Dots Design on Your Own

Good artist needs good tools for them to create. We provide many tools and a clear canvas for you to design your own masterpiece.

32 Colors to Choose

There are 32 colors for you to choose. Pick your favorite rainbow colors and make your work dazzling! 

6 Shapes to Pick

You can choose one shape out of the six. Try all of them and see which is the most special and the most stylish one.

Adjustable Dot & Word Size

Colors and shapes aren't enough for us. We provide two slide bars for you to adjust your dot and word size. Try this feature and let your canvas be starrier.

Changeable Background

A picture with white background is too monotone. Pick up the pen and eraser we give you and embellish your pictures.

Picture Size Setting

Within a mouse click, you can easily change the size of your work. Therefore, you can design both large and small pictures.
